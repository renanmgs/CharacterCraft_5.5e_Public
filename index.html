          Contribute to Character Craft

          Join Our Community

          Character Craft thrives thanks to community contributions. Whether you're translating the app to your language or creating homebrew content, your contributions help make D&D more accessible worldwide.

          Translation

          Help translate Character Craft to your language using the ARB file format. Keep official D&D terms in English as specified in the guidelines.

          Homebrew Content

          Create custom content using our JSON template. Share your homebrew races, classes, spells, and more with the community.

          Guidelines

          • Translation: For the JSON files, keep official D&D terms (Attributes, Skills, Conditions) in English. The ARB can be completely translated.
          • Descriptions: All descriptions can be translated to your language
          • Format: Use the provided ARB and JSON templates
          • Quality: Ensure translations are accurate and culturally appropriate
          • Testing: Test your content before submitting

          Character Craft FAQ

          Does Character Craft 5.5e support the official D&D 2024 rules?

          Yes. Character Craft 5.5e aligns with the 2024 Dungeons & Dragons refresh so you can build heroes with the newest class, spell, and species updates.

          Can I create homebrew content inside the app?

          Yes! You can create any type of content with any type of proprieties!

          Is Character Craft 5.5e free to download?

          The app is free on Google Play, the App Store, and the Microsoft Store. Optional Patreon support helps fund continued updates.

          How do translations work?

          Download the ARB translation file, localize everything and send it to us in our discord! I will add it in the next update of the app.

          Where can I find more documentation?

          For more detailed information, guides, and tutorials, please visit our Documentation page.
