          Contribute to Character Craft

          Join Our Community

          Character Craft thrives thanks to community contributions. Whether you're translating the app to your language or creating homebrew content, your contributions help make D&D more accessible worldwide.

          Translation

          Help translate Character Craft to your language using the ARB file format. Keep official D&D terms in English as specified in the guidelines.

          Homebrew Content

          Create custom content using our JSON template. Share your homebrew races, classes, spells, and more with the community.

          Guidelines

          • Translation: Keep official D&D terms (Attributes, Skills, Conditions, Damage Types) in English
          • Descriptions: All descriptions can be translated to your language
          • Format: Use the provided ARB and JSON templates
          • Quality: Ensure translations are accurate and culturally appropriate
          • Testing: Test your content before submitting
